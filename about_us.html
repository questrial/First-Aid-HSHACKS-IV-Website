About Us

Hello! We are aspiring web developers who are passionate about spreading accessible First Aid and CPR information. We aim to educate anyone and everyone who can access a website on the topic of emergency care and first aid.

Learning CPR and the basics of first aid can greatly aid in helping save someone's life during a crisis. Simply looking over this website and its contents will put you at a much greater advantage when it comes to an emergency. We hope that you can learn more about helping in a crisis through this website and we hope that you can spread this knowledge to others!
